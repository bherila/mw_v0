TIDEWATER UTILITIES, INC. has served customers in southern Delaware since 1964. It provides water service to over 24,000 retail customers throughout Kent, Sussex and New Castle Counties, Delaware. Tidewater serves customers in a variety of communities from small rural operations to large sophisticated systems. With over 180 wells (the most of any entity in Delaware) and over 100 water plants, serving over 240 communities, Tidewater is the largest private water supplier south of the Chesapeake and Delaware Canal. Tidewater has a wholly-owned subsidiary, White Marsh Environmental Systems, Inc., that provides water and wastewater contract operations services. You can visit their website at www.tuiwater.com.
