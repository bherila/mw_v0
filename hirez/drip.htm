Drip & Stock Purchase Information
 		  
 

Q. Does the Company have a Dividend Reinvestment and Stock Purchase Plan (DRIP)?

A. Yes. The Company has a DRIP Plan through which its shareholders can automatically invest all or part of their dividends in and or make direct cash payments to acquire Middlesex Water Common Stock. The Company has direct deposit of dividends whereby dividend payments may be deposited into your checking, savings or money market account. These direct deposits may be credited to your account at any financial institution.

Q. Does Middlesex Water have a direct stock purchase plan?

A. We have a plan that is available to participants in the Dividend Reinvestment Plan. The Company offers the Middlesex Water Company Dividend Reinvestment and Common Stock Purchase Plan, which is designed to provide shareholders of record, or shareholders who

   hold their stock in certificated form, with a convenient method to reinvest cash dividends in the purchase of additional shares of Common Stock and also to purchase shares of the Company's Common Stock through optional cash payments.

If you are not a shareholder, you will need to become a shareholder of record by purchasing at least one share of Common Stock and having a stock certificate issued in your name. You then may enroll in the Plan as described in the Prospectus. Contact the Company at 1500 Ronson Road, Iselin, New Jersey 08830, (732) 634-1500. The Company is periodically required to file with the Securities and Exchange Commission information about the Company. The information includes annual and quarterly reports, known as Forms 10-K and 10-Q, respectively, and proxy statements. The Company also files other information upon the occurrence of significant events, including certain stock issuances.
