Corporate Profile:
Middlesex Water Company is an investor-owned water utility that provides water service in parts of New Jersey and Delaware. Through its subsidiaries, the Company also operates wastewater systems and municipal contract operations. Middlesex Water's common stock trades on the NASDAQ exchange under the symbol MSEX.
