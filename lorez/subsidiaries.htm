Subsidiary Companies
 		  
 

Tidewater Utilities, Inc., (Tidewater) established in 1964, supplies water on a retail basis to more than 30,000 customers in over 410 residential communities and commercial establishments located throughout Kent, Sussex and New Castle Counties in Delaware. Tidewater operates over 88 water plants and more than 175 wells - more than any other entity in the State. Through its affiliated companies in Delaware, Tidewater also serves a wide range of water and wastewater services including consulting, contract operations, maintenance and bulk water supply. You can visit their website at www.tuiwater.com.

Tidewater has a wholly-owned subsidiary, White Marsh Environmental Systems, Inc., that provides water and wastewater contract operations services.   Tidewater Environmental Services, Inc. is an affiliated company providing regulated wastewater utility services to developers and customers.
