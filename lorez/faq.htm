Q. Who should I contact for investor relations information?
A. Inquiries about stock transfer, dividends, dividend reinvestment, missing or lost certificates, change of address notification and other account information should be directed to Bernadette M. Sohler, Director of Communications, (732) 634-1500.

Q. Does the Company file reports with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)?
A. The Company is periodically required to file with the SEC information about the Company. The information includes annual and quarterly reports, known as Forms 10-K and 10-Q, respectively, and proxy statements. The Company also files other information upon the occurrence of significant events, including certain stock issuances. These filings are available in the Corporate Governance section.

Q. Does Middlesex Water issue dividends?
A. The Company has paid cash dividends in varying amounts continually since 1912, and the dividend has been increased in each year since 1973. Its payable dates for Common Stock normally fall in March, June, September and December of each year. Payable dates for Preferred Stock normally fall in February, April, August and November of each year.

Q. How many employees does Middlesex Water Company have?
A. As of December 31, 2003, Middlesex Water Company and its subsidiaries had approximately 209 employees.

Q. What are some of the companies Middlesex Water Company has acquired?
A. Middlesex Water Company acquired Tidewater Utilities, Inc., now headquartered in Odessa, Delaware, in 1992. Public Water Supply Company, Inc., in Millsboro, Delaware, was acquired as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Tidewater Utilities, Inc., in July 1997. Pinelands Water Company, Pinelands Wastewater Company, in Southampton Township, New Jersey, were acquired in 1994, and Fortescue Realty Company, which was subsequently formed Bayview Water Company as of April of 2001.
